- **Oscillating**: Success is neutralized; you advance then regress back (classic problem-solving trap).

The gap creates dynamic tension that naturally resolves toward the vision — *if* you have redesigned the underlying structure so the path of least resistance now leads there. The emphasis is creative orientation (building what you want) rather than reactive problem-solving.
