# Werk 2.0

[[Werk 2.0]] is the placeholder for the next major version of werk. It may deepen werk into an operative substrate for cooperative and agentic work: [[Deep Addressability]], richer [[Session]]s, [[Agent Session]]s, [[Warranty]], [[Provenance]], generic [[Typed Edge]]s, [[Batch Proposal]] review, and a possible [[Werk-Native Agent Harness]].

The central question: how far can werk go while preserving the [[Instrument Boundary]]—remaining an instrument for [[Structural Tension]] rather than becoming an orchestrator or manager?
